Homemade Gnocchi with a Prosciutto Sage Cream Sauce

Time 45 minutes
Serves 4 adults

Ingredients for the Gnocchi:

450 grams of cooked potatoes - cooled and mashed

150 grams for all purpose flour - extra for kneading

3 egg yolks

50 grams of grated parmesan cheese

salt and pepper

Mix all ingredients together until a smooth dough forms, split into 8 equal amounts and roll each into a roll about 1/2 inch thick, cut into 1/2 thick slices. Set aside on a floured plate. Bring large pot of water to boil. Add about a quarter of the gnocchi at a time. Stir gently. Once gnocchi come to surface scoop out of water and add to sacue and gently stir until well coated.

Ingredientsfor the Sauce:

10 min start when water starts to boil

5 sliced of Prosciutto - cut into slices

1 clove of garlic - crushed

10 sage leaves - cut into ribbons

1/2 cup of cream

salt and pepper

dash of paprika and cayenne

Sautee the sliced Prosciutto with the garlic and sage. Once Prosciutto is crispy, about 7 minutes, add the cream and season to taste. Simmer on medium-low heat for 5 min until slightly thickened. Once gnocchi are cooked add to sauce.
Top gnocchi with fresh parmesan and a few leaves of sage. Serve immediatly.

Go ahead, Dig in!
